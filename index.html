Rules & Guidelines

Rule 1: Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect both in-character and out-of-character. Harassment, discrimination, and toxic behavior will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

Rule 2: No Powergaming or Meta-Gaming

Powergaming and meta-gaming are strictly prohibited. This includes forcing actions on other players, unrealistic scenarios, and using out-of-character information in-character.

Rule 3: Stay In Character

Maintain your character at all times during roleplay scenarios. Breaking character disrupts immersion for other players and diminishes the quality of roleplay.

Rule 4: No RDM or VDM

Random Deathmatch (RDM) and Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) are strictly prohibited. All hostile actions must have proper roleplay justification.

Rule 5: Follow Server Lore

All roleplay must adhere to the established server lore and timeline. Creating conflicting backstories or ignoring established events is not permitted.
